Getting Started

Installation Guide

  1. Download the minP AI Browser installation file from our official website.
  2. Run the installer and follow the on-screen instructions.
  3. Choose your installation preferences (default browser, desktop shortcut, etc.).
  4. Click "Install" and wait for the process to complete.
  5. Launch minP AI Browser from your desktop or start menu.

First-Time Setup

When you first launch minP AI Browser, you'll see a welcome screen that guides you through setting up your browser preferences:

  1. Choose your default search engine
  2. Set your privacy preferences
  3. Enable or disable the AI features
  4. Import bookmarks from other browsers (optional)
  5. Set up sync on your device (optional)

You can always change these settings later in the browser's preferences panel.

Understanding the Interface

minP AI Browser's interface is designed to be intuitive and clean:

  • Address Bar: Type URLs or search queries here. The AI will automatically provide suggestions and answers.
  • AI Button: Located at the right side of the address bar. Click to activate the AI assistant for more complex tasks.
  • Tab Bar: Shows all your open tabs. Right-click for additional options.
  • Menu: Access settings, history, bookmarks, and more from the three-dot menu in the top-right corner.
  • Privacy Shield: Shows the current privacy status and lets you enhance protection with a single click.

AI Features

Content Summarization

minP AI Browser can quickly summarize any article or webpage for you:

  1. Click the AI icon in the toolbar while viewing a page, or
  2. Highlight specific text, right-click, and select "Summarize"

All summarization happens locally on your device - no content is sent to external servers.

Research Assistant

The Research Assistant helps you explore topics more deeply:

  • Right-click on any text and select "Research This"
  • The AI will analyze the content and suggest related information
  • Click on any suggestion to open a split-screen view with additional details

This feature is perfect for students, researchers, or anyone looking to understand topics more thoroughly.

Smart Bookmarking

minP AI Browser can organize your bookmarks intelligently:

  • When saving a bookmark, the AI automatically suggests the most appropriate folder based on content
  • Use the "Smart Collections" feature to have the AI automatically group similar sites
  • Ask the AI to find a bookmark by describing its content, even if you don't remember the exact name

Writing Assistant

Get help with writing emails, messages, or content:

  • In any text field, press Alt+W or right-click and select "Writing Assistant"
  • Choose options like "Improve Writing," "Make More Concise," or "Change Tone"
  • The AI will suggest improvements that you can accept or modify

All text processing happens locally on your device to maintain privacy.

Voice Commands

Control your browser with voice:

  • Click the microphone icon or press Alt+V to activate voice mode
  • Speak commands like "Open Gmail," "Search for restaurants," or "Summarize this page"
  • Voice processing happens locally on your device

Privacy & Security

Local Data Processing

minP AI Browser processes all data locally on your device. This means:

  • Your browsing history never leaves your computer
  • AI features analyze content directly on your device
  • No user data is sent to our servers or third parties

Enhanced Privacy Mode

To activate Enhanced Privacy Mode:

  1. Click the shield icon in the toolbar or press Alt+P
  2. Choose your preferred level of protection (Standard, High, Maximum)
  3. Select which features to enable (tracker blocking, fingerprint protection, etc.)

In Maximum protection mode, all tracking is blocked, and websites are prevented from storing any persistent data on your device.

Managing Cookies and Site Data

Control how websites store data:

  1. Open the main menu (three dots in the top-right corner)
  2. Select "Settings" ‚Üí "Privacy & Security"
  3. Under "Cookies and Site Data," choose your preferred settings
  4. Use "Clear Data" to remove stored information

You can also set up automatic clearing of data when you close the browser.

Secure Browsing

minP AI Browser includes several security features:

  • Malicious Site Detection: Warns you about potentially harmful websites
  • Download Scanner: Checks downloaded files for malware (processed locally)
  • Secure Connections: Prioritizes HTTPS connections and notifies you of insecure sites
  • Password Health Check: Analyzes saved passwords for security issues (all analysis happens locally)

Customization

Themes and Appearance

Personalize how your browser looks:

  1. Open Settings (from the main menu)
  2. Select "Appearance"
  3. Choose from pre-installed themes or create a custom one
  4. Adjust colors, font sizes, and density settings

minP AI Browser supports both light and dark modes, with automatic switching based on your system settings or time of day.

Configuring the Toolbar

Add, remove, or rearrange toolbar buttons:

  1. Right-click on the toolbar and select "Customize Toolbar"
  2. Drag and drop buttons to your preferred positions
  3. Add new buttons from the available options
  4. Click "Reset" to restore the default configuration

Keyboard Shortcuts

Create custom keyboard shortcuts:

  1. Go to Settings ‚Üí "Advanced" ‚Üí "Keyboard Shortcuts"
  2. Browse the list of available commands
  3. Click on any command to record a new shortcut
  4. Use the "Reset" button to restore defaults

Start Page Configuration

Customize what you see when opening a new tab:

  • Set a custom homepage or start with a blank page
  • Add shortcuts to frequently visited sites
  • Display news, weather, or other widgets
  • Choose between different layout options

You can also use the AI to create a dynamic start page that adapts to your browsing habits and interests.

Troubleshooting

Frequently Asked Questions

Why is minP AI Browser running slowly? +

Slow performance could be caused by:

  • Too many open tabs (try closing unused tabs)
  • Extension conflicts (try disabling extensions one by one)
  • Large browsing history (clear your history and cache)
  • Hardware limitations (AI features require moderate processing power)

You can also try reducing the AI feature intensity in Settings ‚Üí "Advanced" ‚Üí "Performance."

How do I fix a webpage that won't load correctly? +

Try these steps in order:

  1. Refresh the page (F5 or the refresh button)
  2. Clear the cache for that specific site (right-click on the reload button)
  3. Disable Enhanced Privacy Mode temporarily (some websites rely on tracking for functionality)
  4. Check if the site works in Standard Mode (some sites may have compatibility issues with AI features)
What should I do if the AI features aren't working? +

If AI features aren't responding:

  1. Check if AI features are enabled in Settings ‚Üí "AI Features"
  2. Ensure your device meets the minimum requirements (4GB RAM, modern CPU)
  3. Try restarting the browser
  4. If the issue persists, try reinstalling the AI models in Settings ‚Üí "Advanced" ‚Üí "Repair AI Models"
How can I recover closed tabs? +

To recover recently closed tabs:

  1. Right-click on the tab bar and select "Reopen Closed Tab" or press Ctrl+Shift+T
  2. To see a list of recently closed tabs, click the menu button ‚Üí "History" ‚Üí "Recently Closed"
  3. You can also ask the AI assistant to "recover my closed tabs" for a more intelligent recovery

Reset and Recovery

If you're experiencing persistent issues, you can reset the browser to its default state:

  1. Open Settings ‚Üí "Advanced" ‚Üí "Reset and Recovery"
  2. Choose between "Reset Settings" (keeps your bookmarks and history) or "Full Reset" (removes all data)
  3. Follow the on-screen instructions to complete the process

Note that resetting will remove customizations, but your bookmarks can be backed up first.

Extensions

Finding and Installing Extensions

minP AI Browser supports a wide range of extensions:

  1. Open the main menu and select "Extensions" or press Alt+E
  2. Click "Browse Extensions" to open the Extension Store
  3. Search for extensions by name or category
  4. Click "Install" on any extension you want to add

You can also install extensions from third-party sources by dragging the extension file into the Extensions page.

Managing Extensions

Control your installed extensions:

  • Enable/disable extensions by toggling the switch next to each one
  • Click on an extension for detailed settings
  • Use the "Remove" button to uninstall an extension
  • Pin extensions to the toolbar for easy access

Extension Privacy

Review extension permissions:

  • Each extension shows what permissions it requires
  • Use "Details" to see exactly what data an extension can access
  • Enable "Extension Isolation" in Privacy Settings to limit extension access to your data
  • Use "Privacy Mode" to temporarily disable extensions that might compromise privacy

minP AI Browser actively monitors extensions for suspicious behavior and alerts you to potential privacy concerns.
