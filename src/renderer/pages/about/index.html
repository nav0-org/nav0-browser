Our Story

How we're changing the browsing experience

minP AI Browser was born from a simple idea: the web browsing experience could be better with AI integration. Our founders, a team of passionate web developers and AI specialists, set out to create a browser that prioritizes speed, security, and intelligent user experience above all else.

What started as a small project has grown into a full-featured browser used by thousands of people around the world. We're committed to building a product that respects your privacy, enhances your productivity with AI assistance, and makes browsing the web a joy.

Our journey is just beginning, and we're excited to have you along for the ride.
